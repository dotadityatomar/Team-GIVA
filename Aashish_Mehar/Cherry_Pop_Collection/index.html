Love Blossoms: Celebrating Valentine's Day with GIVA ▼
Valentine's Day, a day that paints the town in shades of love and passion, has its roots in a tale of undying romance. Legend has it that during the reign of Roman Emperor Claudius II, the emperor prohibited marriage for soldiers to keep them focused on battle. Despite this decree, a brave priest named Valentine defied the order and continued to unite young lovers in matrimony. Eventually discovered and sentenced to death, Valentine left a lasting legacy by falling in love with the jailor's daughter and signing off his final letters as "Your Valentine."

Jewellery: A Grand Statement of Love ▼

Jewellery not only adds a touch of elegance but also symbolizes commitment, making it a heart-melting gesture for the recipient.
